Movies that I like.

By Haris Ahmad

List of the movies that I liked watching:

Spider Man 2

My favourite movie since childhood, always loved the back story and the evolution of the original Peter Parker in the film franchise. Toby Maguire played the role very well.

The Dark Knight Rises

Remarkable movie directed by renowned Christopher Nolan. The visuals and stunts were absolutely remarkable.

Air Force One

Action packed thriller led by Harrison Ford while playing the role of US President. An all time classic 90s action.
